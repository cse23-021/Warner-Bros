Warner Bros. Entertainment inc

Warner Bros is an American film and entertainment studio headquartered at the Warner Bros studios complex in Burbank california. The company is a huge figure in the entertainment industry with multiple subsidaries.Its subsidaries are all independent company that all have their own speciality entertainment production such as animation, television and video games.

Founders

The company was founded by four brothers Harry, Albert, Sam and Jack Warner in 1923.

The warner brothers: Albert, Jack, Harry and Sam

Warner Bros Subsidiary

Warner Bros Entertainment operates three primary business segments called divisions. There is the Motion Picture Group, Television and other entertainment assets. Each one of this divisions houses different film productions units.

Motion Picture Group

  • Warner Bros.Pictures
  • New line Cinema
  • DC Studios
  • Warner Bros. Pictures Animation
  • Castle Rock Entertainment
  • Spyglass Media Group
  • Flagship Entertainment Group

Television Group

  • Warner Bros. Television Studios
