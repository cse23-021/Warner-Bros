The company was established in the early 1900s by the Warner brothers Jack, Sam, Harry and Albert thus the name Warner Bros.

This company as showm by the picture is home to some of the world's most loved fictional characters. It has been providing quality entertainment ot viewers for over a century and promises to contunue on with such quality works for future decades to come.
